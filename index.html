About Unlabelled

Unlabelled began with a single question: What if clothing could speak when you couldn’t?

As students, we carry invisible weight — stress, comparison, judgement, and the pressure to be “perfect.” Unlabelled was created to help you release those labels and reclaim your story.

Every hoodie, every tee, and every QR code is crafted to help you feel grounded, encouraged, and seen — even on days when words are hard.

Why QR Codes?

  • Scan quietly with your phone — no one has to know.
  • Instant affirmations tailored to stress, anxiety, and self-doubt.
  • Mini grounding exercises you can do in under one minute.
  • Gentle reminders that you are more than the labels people put on you.

Tools for Stressful College Days

5 Affirmations for Stressful College Days

When everything piles up — midterms, projects, group work, social life — it can feel like too much. These affirmations are designed to meet you where you are:

  • “I can take this one step at a time.”
  • “I choose progress, not perfection.”
  • “My feelings are valid.”
  • “I am learning and growing every day.”
  • “I deserve rest.”

Why We Added QR Codes to Clothing

QR codes turn your hoodie into a quiet support system that meets you on your walk to class, in the library, or during a late-night study session. One scan, one breath, one reminder: you’re not alone.

Baby Pink & Navy Blue: Color Psychology

Baby Pink symbolizes softness, warmth, and emotional safety. Navy Blue brings grounding, strength, and trust. Together, they reflect exactly what Unlabelled stands for — calm, steady, and deeply human.
